The Innovation: Round-Robin Proof of Authority

Our system uses a custom consensus mechanism that is efficient, fork-free, and highly fault-tolerant.

How It Works: A Fault-Tolerant Cycle

Leader 1 Creates Block
Leader 2 Creates Block
Leader 3 Fails (Timeout!)
Leader 4 Creates Block

The network automatically detects the failed node, skips its turn, and continues the cycle, ensuring the blockchain **never halts**.
